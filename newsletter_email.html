OneSource

Student level data to make informed decisions towards student success

Our integrated data system, OneSource, continues to evolve with new customizations and data, with close to 30,000 students onboarded.

During the last quarter we have been working to add data from our latest onboarded district, Santa Cruz City Schools.

Additionally, we have been working with the Curriculum and Instruction team to add customizations such as new dashboards and filters, in order to better serve the needs of our users.

For example, we have added a new Multilingual Learners Dashboard that shows the numbers of students in different classification statuses and how they perform in state assessments by classification status. Look at the two dashboards below for OneSource users and non-OneSource users.
